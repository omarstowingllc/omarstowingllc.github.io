How it works

Simple pickup, honest costs, meaningful impact.

We make the process straightforward: request a quote, confirm the car location, schedule pickup, remove the car, cover the expenses of running the service, and direct 20% of the remaining profit to CureDuchenne.

Why it matters

Every eligible vehicle helps move money toward research and support.

Your old car becomes more than a junk removal task. It becomes a channel for support, helping families and research efforts connected to Duchenne muscular dystrophy while we keep a fair share to cover the business. Free removal depends on location.
